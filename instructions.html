Phase 1 — In the Datacenter (Phone)

Capturing the rack

Do this for each rack before moving to the next one.

1

Open the Field Capture app on your phone

Go to the URL above in Chrome or Safari. Tap Add to Home Screen from your browser menu for quicker access next time.

2

Tap + to start a new rack survey

Enter the Rack ID exactly as labelled on the rack (e.g. R04, A-02-L). Add a location note if helpful (e.g. "Level 2 Aisle B").

3

Take rack overview photos

Tap the two photo slots to take a wide shot of the full rack from both sides.

4

Add each piece of equipment

Tap Add Equipment Item for each server, switch, UPS, or other device. For each item:

  • Enter the Start U and End U position (check the rack U markings)
  • Select the category (Server, Network Switch, UPS, etc.)
  • Take one or more close-up photos of the front label and serial number tag — the more readable the better
  • Add a note if needed (hostname sticker, asset tag)
📸 Photo tips for best results Make sure the label is in focus and well-lit. Use your phone torch if the rack is dark. Include the serial number barcode if there is one. It's fine to take multiple photos of the same item from different angles.
⚠️ Don't skip the U position Always enter the Start U before saving — this is what tells the system exactly where in the rack each item sits. Check the metal U markings on the rack rail.
5

Export the session when the rack is done

Tap Export (top right) → Download Session JSON. This saves a file to your phone. Send it to yourself via email, Slack, or WhatsApp — whatever you use.

6

Repeat for the next rack

Tap the back arrow to return to the home screen, then tap + to start a new survey for the next rack. Each rack is a separate session.

Phase 2 — Back at Your Desk (Laptop)

Processing into Excel

Do this after you've finished all the racks, or at the end of each day.

1

Open the Desktop Scanner on your laptop

Go to the desktop URL above. Paste your Power Automate Flow URL in the field at the top — ask your team lead if you don't have it.

2

Import your session files

Click Import Field Session and select the JSON file you exported from your phone. Repeat for each rack session. They'll all appear as cards on screen.

3

Click Scan on each session

The AI reads each photo and extracts the part number and serial number. The rack number, U position, and category you entered in the field are already filled in — the AI only needs to read the labels.

4

Review and correct the table

Check any rows where the part number or serial number looks wrong or is blank. You can click any cell to edit it directly. If a serial number couldn't be read, go back to your photo or check the equipment physically.

5

Export to Excel

Click Export to Excel. The file downloads as rack-assets-YYYY-MM-DD.xlsx with all racks, U positions, categories, part numbers, and serial numbers.

Before you go in

Pre-survey checklist

Phone charged to at least 50%
Field Capture app bookmarked or on home screen
Know the rack IDs you're surveying today
Torch / flashlight available (phone torch is fine)
Enough phone storage for photos (each session ~50–100 MB)
